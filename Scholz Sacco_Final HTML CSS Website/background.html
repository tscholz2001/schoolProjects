Learn about our Head Chef at The Marine Room!


Pierre Passard is a world famous chef that has been with us at the marine room for the last 3 years. In order to master his craft, he traveled all around the world to incorporate different cultures into his cooking. Pierre has created everything on the menu based on his experiences and what he has learned from other chefs. Pierre has worked with the famous Gordon Ramsay and has won countless awards such as the James Beard Foundation Award.
